The Sweat Lodge

We are a community with connections to the Blood Reserve, Canada, holding sweatlodges in rural South Oxfordshire that honour the lineage of the Blackfoot Sundance tradition.

The ceremony was brought to this land around the year 2000, by a Blackfoot Sundance leader, elder and lodge leader who passed on the tradition over several years, and we now have community members attending the annual Sundance ceremonies in Canada.

The sweat lodge is an ancient earth based medicine tradition calling on mother earth, father sky and the four directions to come and help us in a sacred way.

We hold a Mixed community lodge every month, in all weathers, as well as men's and women's lodges whenever possible.
